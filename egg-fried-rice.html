Egg Fried Rice

A simple rice recipe that uses leftovers and cooks fast in one pan.

Recipe Details

Preparation Time: 20 minutes

Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 2 tbsp chopped onion
  • 2 tbsp chopped carrot or capsicum
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan and scramble the eggs. Set aside.
  2. In the same pan, cook onion and vegetables for 2 minutes.
  3. Add cooked rice and mix well.
  4. Stir in soy sauce, salt, and pepper.
  5. Add scrambled eggs back, mix, and serve warm.
