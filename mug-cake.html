2-Minute Mug Cake

A quick sweet fix with basic ingredients and no oven required.

Recipe Details

Preparation Time: 7 minutes

Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 4 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 3 tbsp milk
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1/4 tsp baking powder

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Mix flour, sugar, cocoa powder, and baking powder in a mug.
  2. Add milk and oil, then stir until smooth.
  3. Microwave for 60 to 90 seconds.
  4. Let it rest for 1 minute before eating.
  5. Top with chocolate chips or nuts if available.
