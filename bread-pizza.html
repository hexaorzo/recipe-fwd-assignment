Bread Pizza

No-oven pizza made with bread, perfect for quick hostel snacks.

Recipe Details

Preparation Time: 18 minutes

Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 4 bread slices
  • 4 tbsp pizza sauce or ketchup
  • 1/2 cup grated cheese
  • 2 tbsp chopped onion
  • 2 tbsp chopped capsicum
  • 1 tsp chili flakes and oregano

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Spread pizza sauce over each bread slice.
  2. Add onion and capsicum on top.
  3. Sprinkle grated cheese and seasonings.
  4. Toast on a covered pan or oven until cheese melts.
  5. Slice and serve hot.
