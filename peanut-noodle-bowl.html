Peanut Noodle Bowl

Creamy and spicy noodles with simple ingredients and quick prep.

Recipe Details

Preparation Time: 20 minutes

Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

  • 1 packet noodles
  • 2 tbsp peanut butter
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp chili sauce
  • 1/2 tsp sugar
  • 2 tbsp warm water
  • Spring onion for garnish (optional)

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Boil noodles according to packet instructions and drain.
  2. Mix peanut butter, soy sauce, chili sauce, sugar, and warm water.
  3. Toss hot noodles in the sauce until coated.
  4. Add a little water if the sauce is too thick.
  5. Garnish with spring onion and serve.
