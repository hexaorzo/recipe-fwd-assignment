One-Pot Maggi

Quick, warm, and filling noodles made with basic hostel pantry ingredients.

Recipe Details

Preparation Time: 15 minutes

Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 1 packet instant noodles
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 tsp oil
  • 1/4 cup chopped onion
  • 2 tbsp mixed vegetables (optional)
  • Noodle masala sachet

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a small pan and saute onion for 1 minute.
  2. Add vegetables and cook for another minute.
  3. Pour in water and bring it to a boil.
  4. Add noodles and masala, then cook for 2 to 3 minutes.
  5. Stir well and serve hot.
